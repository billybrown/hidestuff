Hiding Stuff

Different ways to hide content on the web

Why do we sometimes want to hide stuff?

  • We are on a small screen and have limited realestate
  • We want to get to the content as quickly as possible, but still give motivated users access to additionl options or information
  • We don't want to obscure our design or content with other content
